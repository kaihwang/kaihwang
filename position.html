Open positions

Postdoc

Our lab has one NIH funded postdoctoral position available. Potential research topics include brain network mechanisms of cognitive control and functions of the human thalamocortical system. Our research combines multimodal neuroimaging (EEG and fMRI), TMS, and lesion studies with network neuroscience approaches. Details can be found here. Within these broad themes, specific topics will be organized in accordance to the applicants' skills and interests. We are affiliated with the cognitive control research collaborative, which offers a collaborative research environment with access to research-dedicated 3T and 7T MRI systems, TMS, EEG, neurosurgery patients, and a large lesion patient registry.

Required Qualifications:

  • PhD in Psychology, Neuroscience, or other related disciplines.
  • Inherent interest in cognitive control, brain connectivity, and neural oscillations.
  • Background in human neuroscience and/or cognitive psychology experimental approaches.
  • Extensive experience with neuroimaging research (fMRI, EEG/MEG).
  • Strong Programming skills (Matlab or Python)

    • To apply please email a CV and cover letter to kai-hwang@uiowa.edu.
