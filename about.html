Welcome!

Research in the Hwang lab focuses on brain network mechanisms of cognitive control and its developmental process. You can learn more about our work by reading the research page or publications.

We are currently recruiting talented post-docs, graduate students, and research assistants. Please contact Kai (kai-hwang@uiowa.edu) if you are interested.
